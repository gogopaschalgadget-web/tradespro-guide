✓ Home Plumbing Inspection Checklist

The Complete Guide to Identifying Plumbing Issues

💡 Tip: Print this checklist and use it for your home inspection. You can also check items digitally below.
🚰 Kitchen Plumbing
Check pipes and shut-off valves for drips
🚿 Bathrooms
A running toilet can waste 200+ gallons per day
🔥 Water Heater & Main
Should have discharge tube to prevent scalding
🌱 Outdoor & Irrigation
Check valves, connectors, and sprinkler heads
🚨 Emergency Shut-Offs & Safety

Pro Tip: Perform this inspection quarterly to catch problems early. Regular maintenance can save thousands in repair costs.
