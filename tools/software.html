Software That Grows With Your Business

Managing a contracting business involves much more than the physical work - you need systems for scheduling, billing, customer relationships, and project tracking. The right software eliminates administrative headaches, helps you win more jobs, and keeps your team organized. We've selected business software specifically designed for contractors and proven by thousands of professionals.

Pro Pick 📱

ServiceTitan

All-in-one platform for scheduling, dispatching, invoicing, and customer management. Built specifically for home service contractors with mobile apps.

★★★★★ (2,800+ reviews)
Best for: Plumbers, HVAC, electricians and multi-trade companies. Scales from solo operators to large teams.
Pricing: Custom pricing based on team size and features (typically $500-3000+/month)
Visit ServiceTitan
Pro Pick 📅

Jobber

User-friendly job management software with scheduling, mobile dispatch, estimates, invoicing, and customer database. Designed for simplicity.

★★★★★ (2,100+ reviews)
Best for: Small to mid-sized contracting businesses. Easier learning curve than enterprise platforms.
Pricing: Starting at $600/month for 3 users, scales up to $2000+ for larger teams
Visit Jobber
🏠

Housecall Pro

Affordable, easy-to-use platform for scheduling, invoicing, and customer management. Mobile app keeps technicians connected with real-time job details.

★★★★☆ (1,900+ reviews)
Best for: Solo operators and small teams looking for affordability. Great for service-based contractors.
Pricing: Plans starting at $40/month, scales to $200+ for larger operations with multiple users
Visit Housecall Pro
💰

QuickBooks for Contractors

Industry-specific accounting software with invoicing, expense tracking, and job costing. Integrates with other tools for financial visibility.

★★★★☆ (3,200+ reviews)
Best for: Businesses needing robust accounting, tax preparation, and reporting. Contractors of all sizes.
Pricing: Starts at $30/month for basic, up to $200+ for advanced features and integrations
Visit QuickBooks
📸

CompanyCam

Photo documentation platform for job sites and client communication. Organize photos by project, share with clients, and maintain visual records.

★★★★★ (1,600+ reviews)
Best for: Any contractor wanting professional photo documentation. Essential for before/after and approval.
Pricing: Starting at $60/month for single user, team plans from $300+/month
Visit CompanyCam
🏗️

Buildertrend

Comprehensive project management platform for contractors and builders. Handles planning, scheduling, budgeting, punch lists, and owner communication.

★★★★☆ (1,400+ reviews)
Best for: General contractors, builders, and larger remodeling companies. Excellent for multi-phase projects.
Pricing: Custom pricing based on features needed, typically $150-500+ per month
Visit Buildertrend
