Electrical Safety Checklist: Protect Your Home

By Mike Johnson March 18, 2026 4 min read

Electrical Safety Basics

Electrical hazards cause thousands of home fires annually. Regular safety checks prevent disasters.

Home Safety Inspection

Visual Inspection

  • Check outlets for discoloration or scorch marks
  • Look for flickering lights
  • Inspect cords for damage
  • Verify GFCIs in kitchens, bathrooms
  • Test circuit breakers monthly

Red Flags

Call a licensed electrician immediately if you notice:

  • Burning smell from outlets
  • Frequent circuit breaker trips
  • Sparks from outlets
  • Outlets or switches that are hot to touch
  • Outdated wiring (knob and tube, aluminum)

Preventive Measures

  1. Install GFCI outlets (bathrooms, kitchen, garage)
  2. Use surge protectors for expensive equipment
  3. Don't overload circuits
  4. Replace old two-prong outlets with grounded three-prong
  5. Have professional inspection every 5 years
