HVAC Maintenance Guide: Keep Your System Running

By Jane Doe March 19, 2026 6 min read

HVAC System Overview

Your HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) system is critical for maintaining comfort. Regular maintenance prevents breakdowns and extends system life by 10+ years.

Seasonal Maintenance Schedule

Spring (Before AC Season)

  • Replace air filters
  • Clean outdoor AC unit
  • Test AC system before peak season
  • Check refrigerant levels

Fall (Before Heating Season)

  • Replace air filters again
  • Test heating system
  • Inspect ductwork for leaks
  • Clean vents and registers

Energy Efficiency Tips

Proper maintenance saves 5-15% on energy costs:

  1. Change filters every 1-3 months
  2. Program thermostat to 68°F in winter, 78°F in summer
  3. Seal leaks in ductwork
  4. Keep outdoor unit clear of debris
  5. Use ceiling fans to improve air circulation

When to Call a Professional

  • Strange noises from furnace or AC
  • Inconsistent temperature between rooms
  • High energy bills without explanation
  • Age over 15 years (consider replacement)
